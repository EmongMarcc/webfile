PRODUCERS:

Dubai Film offers a full production service for both regional and international productions of TV Commercials, Digital Content, Feature Films, Documentaries, Facilitation and TV Series

We guarantee your production requirements will be met with exceptionally efficient service, dedicated cost awareness and a passionate resolve for the end product.

Dubai Film's producers are world renowned for their speedy and efficient ability to source and access locations, as well as, apply for aerial and ground permits within the UAE on your behalf

STOCK FOOTAGE:

Dubai Film has the largest library of UAE based stock footage in the entire region. Get ready to discover a world of mind-blowing high definition stock videos and footage, fully licensed for commercial use, these stock footages are all shot from helicopter using 6-axis stabilized gimbal systems in 4K definition, which will inspire you and let you create an impression that best delivers your product, brand or service.

TRACKING, AERIAL & UNDERWATER:
